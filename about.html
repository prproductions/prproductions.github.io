Our Team

About Us

Founded in 1995, On-Time has been a firmly established player in the Indian IT industry, providing wide spectrum of technology solutions & services to a diverse customer base. Operating in Mumbai and with an experienced team in almost every vertical, we have the ability to provide our customers a blend of optimal functionality, value for money, commitment and flexibility.
Keeping up with the various changes in technology, On-Time has spread its wings into the area of Automation and IOT based solutions.

About Our CEO

Having founded the company in 1995, Mr. Tejas Shroff has been a pioneer in the Information Technology industry. Having a passion to continuously find new, innovative and efficient solutions, he has been able to guide the company to cater to not only basic IT infrastructure requirements, but also cater to other solutions (Whether it be software, networking, etc). With his guidance and experience, the company also provides consultancy services to Hospitals, Clinics, Day-care Centres and other setups in Healthcare Domain with regard to revamping their I.T. infrastructure to meet the current technology needs or to setup altogether a new I.T. infrastructure to meet the needs of a new Setup.

About Our Management

With a blend of 25 years of experience and young innovative minds, the management team at On-Time has been able to successfully evolve in other various verticals of the technology sector. Through the support of Mr. Himanshu Mehta, Mr. Rahul Shroff and Mrs. Lata Shroff, the company has been able to branch out into 3 new verticals since 2013. With an experience of over 3 years, On-Time now caters to Technology based Security Solutions and different forms of Automation. Having successful tie-ups and associations with the leaders in each vertical, the support and quality the On-Time has been providing over the last 23 years has not deteriorated.
